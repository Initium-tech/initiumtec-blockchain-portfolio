# The CONCHO Conservation Pledge (DRAFT — not yet in effect)

> Status: DRAFT. Takes effect only at mainnet launch, and only after a
> conservation partner has confirmed in writing. Until then CONCHO exists
> solely on the Sepolia test network and has no monetary value.

## The pledge

1. **50% of all liquidity-provider trading fees** earned by the CONCHO team's
   LP position will be donated to conservation of the sapo concho
   (*Peltophryne lemur*), Puerto Rico's only native toad.
2. **15% of the total token supply** is reserved in a Conservation Fund
   wallet, published at launch, to be sold only in pre-announced tranches
   whose proceeds are donated in full.
3. **Every donation is published**: transaction hash, amount, date, and
   recipient, posted on the CONCHO website and social channels. On-chain
   proof, not promises.
4. **Donations happen quarterly**, on a published schedule, starting the
   first full quarter after launch.

## The partner

Target partner: the Puerto Rican Crested Toad Conservancy (crestedtoad.org)
or an equivalent recognized program (AZA species survival plan participants).

**Rule: no partner's name appears in any public CONCHO material until they
have agreed in writing.** Outreach email draft: `conservancy-outreach-email.md`.
If no partnership is secured, donations go to a recognized amphibian
conservation program and the pledge language says exactly that.

## What this pledge is not

- Not an investment promise. CONCHO is a community meme token; its price can
  go to zero. Nothing here creates any expectation of profit.
- Not charity-washing. If we ever fail to publish a scheduled donation,
  holders should call it out — the transaction record makes that possible.
