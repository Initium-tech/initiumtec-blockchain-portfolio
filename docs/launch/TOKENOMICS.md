# CONCHO Tokenomics (DRAFT — proposal for Rafael's approval)

Total supply: **100,000,000,000 CONCHO** — fixed at deployment. The contract
has no mint function, no owner, no pause, no blacklist, and no transfer taxes.
Nobody — including the team — can change these numbers after launch.
(Mainnet launch = a fresh deployment; the current Sepolia token is a rehearsal.)

## Allocation

| Bucket | % | Amount | Rules |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Liquidity pool** | 50% | 50B | Paired with ETH at launch; **LP position locked 12 months** (lock tx published) |
| **Community** | 20% | 20B | Airdrops, contests, contributor rewards — distributed over the first year |
| **Conservation Fund** | 15% | 15B | Separate published wallet; sold only in pre-announced tranches, proceeds donated in full (see PLEDGE.md) |
| **Team** | 10% | 10B | 12-month linear vesting; any sale announced ≥72h in advance |
| **Operations** | 5% | 5B | Listings, tooling, and costs; spends disclosed monthly |

## Standing commitments

- Every wallet above is named and published at launch day, before any buying
  is possible.
- The team never sells unannounced. One violation of this rule should end
  your trust in the project — that's the point of writing it down.
- 50% of team LP fees → conservation donations, quarterly, on-chain proof.
- No "no promises" fine print trickery: CONCHO is a meme token. It is not an
  investment, nobody should buy it expecting profit, and its price can go to
  zero. Buy it because the toad is worth saving and the meme is fun.
